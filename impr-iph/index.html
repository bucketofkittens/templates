Dashboard

Dashboard shows your life as a set of simple indicators. You can see your current status as well as measure and track the impact of your achievements in every aspect of your life.

Life profile

Profile is your current self-assessment. It encompasses five areas of life: Myself, Relationships, Career, Lifestyle and Spirituality. Identify where you currently are on the scale for each indicator by considering each area of your life.

Goals

Goals mirror the same five areas of life included in your Profile. Improve underperforming areas of your Profile by setting clear action-oriented Goals. Goals translate your dreams into actions and help you stay focused.

Tasks

Record all the tasks you need to accomplish. Map them back to Goals and stay on track achieving your dreams. Check off the tasks when done and enjoy the sense of accomplishment.

Notes

An extra tool to help you stay organized and add some fun. Notes map back to Tasks and Goals and can include text, pictures and other content that can help you achieve your targets.
